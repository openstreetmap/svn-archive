More than just a map renderer...

  • Freemap aims to provide a range of interactive features on top of the base OpenStreetMap data
  • Users can annotate maps e.g.
    • Blockages (barbed wire, boggy paths, killer cows etc)
    • Directions at points where paths are hard to follow
    • Interesting views or particularly aesthetically-appealing paths
  • Users will be able to contribute walking routes, and again either share those with others or keep them personal

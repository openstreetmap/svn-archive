Freemap and OpenStreetMap

  • About a year after I started Freemap, I became aware of Steve Coast's OpenStreetMap project, which was accomplishing a similar aim but (at the time) focused on city streetmaps
  • It became apparent that it would make sense to have one centralised database for free mapping data, so I approached Steve about collaboration
  • The conclusion was that Freemap data would be stored in OSM's database, then Freemap would pull out the data from OSM and render it in its own style
